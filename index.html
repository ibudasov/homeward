What exactly do we do?

1
Define scope

Establish certification targets, select frameworks (PCI/DSS, SOC2, NIS2, ISO27k) and map all in-scope systems, processes and stakeholders.

2
Assess and gap

Perform a gap analysis against selected requirements, document current controls, and prioritize remediation tasks.

3
Implement controls

Apply technical, organizational, and procedural controls (risk management, access, logging, encryptions, incident response) to close identified gaps.

4
Validate readiness

Run internal audits, staff training, and pre-assessment checks; adjust controls and evidence before external audit.

5
Certify and maintain

Undergo official assessment, obtain certification, and operationalize continuous monitoring and improvement cycles.

Our Clients' Stories


FYI With respect for privacy of our clients we changed the names of the companies

PCI/DSS preparation for UkrCredit NIS2 preparation for Eletruck PCI/DSS maintenance for Aggrepay SOC2 for Bruno

UkrCredit is a Ukrainian fintech company that is certified as a PCI/DSS card processor.

We collaborated with them on understanding the scope, reducing it to bare minimum in order to achieve the smallest possible surface of attack.

After that we reviewed all the digital assets of the company in the scope and created a remediation plan. During the implementation phase we are suppporting UkrCredit and looking forward for the final assesment.

Eletruck — NIS2-compliant critical European infrastructure provider.

We worked on network security hardening: Ingress and Egress traffic unification, East-West traffic scanning and segregation.

Since the company operates critical infrastructure, we identified what is considered staying operational in disasterous situation, identified critical resources and set them up in High Availability manner. The project was completed with a disaster simulation over production environment.

Apart from that we hardened security of all the critical public company services in order to be protected from most popular attacks like DDoS, ransomware, OWASP, etc.

Aggrepay — PCI/DSS compliant credit card processor and payment aggregator.

We helped Aggrepay to maintain their PCI/DSS compliance over almost 3 years, which means 3 recurring assessments: communication with the assessing company, remediation of identified issues, stakeholder management.

Mostly worked on the maintenence and development of the compliant parts of the system, developing new features in a secure way

Bruno — SOC2-compliant AI health coach.

In order to operate on US and EU markets, Bruno needed to meet strict SOC2 compliance requirements.

We helped them to achieve that by working on the gap analysis and implementation of the required controls, documentation and evidence collection.

Due to use of AI, we also implemented additional security measures to ensure the protection of sensitive data.
